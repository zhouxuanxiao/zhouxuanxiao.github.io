👨‍🎓**Biography**<br>
Zhouxuan Xiao received the B.E. and M.S. degree from the Chang'an University, Xi'an, China, in 2017 and 2020, respectively. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in the School of Geospatial Engineering and Science, Sun Yat-sen University, Zhuhai, China. His research interests include 2D/3D, image processing and planetary remote sensing. More info can be found on his **[CV](ZhouxuanXiao_CV_2024v1.pdf)**<br>
